Train Prototype

A puzzle-oriented rail-system prototype focused on custom map creation, rail validation, and train pathfinding. The work emphasised flexible level design and logical train behaviour.

  • Custom grid-based railroad creation and overlap handling for junctions.
  • Rail placement, rotation, and track section processing into a full network.
  • Validation of carriage ordering and puzzle-specific yard layouts.
  • Train pathing and steering at junctions with attachment and detachment support.
