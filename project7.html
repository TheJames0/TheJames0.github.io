Car Customization Tool 3JS

The goal of this project were to make a simple interactive car customization tool which was usable on the web. The main objectives were:

  • Render 3D car models and parts
  • Ability to modify parts or change aspects of the car

This project mainly relied on OOP principles to reduce the difficulty of adding new parts to the correct place using Parts and Sockets
