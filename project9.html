Animal Tycoon

A polished tycoon experience built around animal management, progression, and player feedback. The project covered core systems for building, resource flow, AI behaviour, and long-term player goals.

  • Workstation building and resource cost systems.
  • Animal AI behaviour, wandering, and delivery routines.
  • Milestone and levelling progression.
  • Tycoon statistics, creature collection, and hiring systems.
  • Trader deals, contract systems, and completion prompts.
  • UI prompts and controller support with input hints and remapping.
