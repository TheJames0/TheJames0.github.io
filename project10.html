CCTV Horror - Realm Paradox

A surveillance horror project focused on game design, level creation, and a paradox-driven encounter system. The work included core GDD, scene building, lighting, gameplay balancing, and a flexible narrative toolkit.

  • GDD and overall game design for the project.
  • Level design, environment modelling, props, and lighting for multiple scenes.
  • Encounter and paradox systems that trigger player investigation and reporting.
  • Balancing encounter timing, occurrence types, and edge-case handling.
  • Custom audio playback for music and sound effects.
  • Inventory and item support for story progression and paradox detection.
  • Shaders and custom rendering logic for tools such as black light and thermal effects.
  • A camera item for capturing hidden or out-of-view objects.
